Sequential Fortuna

  • The Goal: Be the most prosperous survivor after 3 rounds.
  • The Board: 12 Arms, each with 3 Ring Slots (Outer, Mid, Inner).
  • Betting: Place bets on any arm. Costs increase each round: Round 1 costs 1€, Round 2 costs 2€, and Round 3 costs 4€. Each arm can only have ONE bet per round.
  • Must Engage: You MUST place at least one bet per round or you are ELIMINATED.
  • House Fill: Once betting stops, the House fills all vacant slots for the round.
  • The Roll: Two dice are rolled. Payouts are triggered for Die 1, Die 2, and the Sum.
  • Reflections: If a hit number is already out, it reflects forward (Current + Die) until it hits or misses.
  • Payouts: The Table Pot is split. Owners of the hit numbers claim the shares.
