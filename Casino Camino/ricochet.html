Ricochet Elimination

  • Goal: Be the LAST SURVIVOR to claim the entire Pot.
  • Turns: Players take turns spinning the wheel.
  • Winner Takes All: Survive alone to win. If everyone dies, the House keeps the pot.

Symbol Legend

  • ☠️ Skull: Instant Elimination.
  • € Coin: Safe. Adds money to the pot (increases each round).
  • €€ Jackpot: Double reward! Adds double the current round reward.
  • ❤️ Heart: Extra Life. Protects you from one elimination.
  • ⏳ Hourglass: Speed Frozen. Locks the wheel at base speed for next turn.
  • 🧊 Ice Cube: Size Frozen. Permanent 1-round advantage on tile size.
  • 🔍 Glass: True Sight. Allows you to see through the wooden covers.
