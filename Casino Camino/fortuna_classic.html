Fortuna House Rules

  • Objective: Bet on the sum of two dice.
  • Elimination: Once a sum is rolled, it is REMOVED from the game.
  • Doubles (=): Bet on any pair (1-1, 2-2, etc.).
  • Safety Colors:
    Green = Safe (3 Combinations)
    Orange = Medium (2 Combinations)
    Red = Risky (1 Combination)
    Purple = Special (Doubles)

Behind The Odds

Payouts are calculated dynamically based on the Real Probability of your dice combination and the number of turns you purchase.

  • Dynamic Pricing: Rare outcomes (like 2, 12, or Doubles) pay significantly more than common sums (6, 7, 8).
  • Turn Cost: Buying more turns increases your chance to win, but lowers the final payout.
  • House Edge: A fixed 8% House Edge is applied to all payouts to ensure the Casino always wins in the long run.
  • Fair Play: Bets that would payout less than the entry fee (€100) are automatically disabled.
