2. Round Structure

The game lasts 5 Rounds. Hand capacity scales up by 1 card each round.

  1. Turns: Players TAKE TURNS to bet. You must CALL (match), RAISE (increase), or FOLD.
  2. Resolution: After the last turn, the best hand takes the Pot.
  3. Discard & Draw: After each round, played cards are discarded. You keep your unplayed hand and draw cards to reach your capacity.

3. Victory & Tie-Breakers

The winner is the player with the Most Cash at the end of Round 5!

Split Pots: If two or more commanders deploy hands of identical strength, the Pot is divided equally. Any remaining coin ($1) is awarded to the first tied commander in the turn order.

Hierarchy (Superior Volume Prevails): A configuration deploying a greater number of cards universally overcomes one deploying fewer.

  • Tier 1: 5 of a Rank, Pure Suit, Coalition (Alliance), or Campaign (Sequence)
  • Tier 2: 4 of a Rank, Pure, Coalition, or Campaign
  • Tier 3: 3 of a Rank, Pure, Coalition, or Campaign
  • Tier 4: Pairs
  • Tier 5 (Skirmishes): Unmatched cards. Pure (Product x 2), Coalition (Product), Divided (Sum). Rank 1 imitates the highest card in the hand.
